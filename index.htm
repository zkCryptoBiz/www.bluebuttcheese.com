How to buy

1
Create a wallet

Download Phantom wallet or your wallet of choice from the app store for free. Desktop users, download the Google Chrome extension by going to the Phantom app.

2
Get some sol

Have SOL in your wallet to switch to OBB. If you don’t have any SOL, you can buy directly on Phantom, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and add it to your wallet.

3
Go to Raydium

Connect to Raydium or Jupiter. Go to raydium.io or jup.ag inside your Phantom app. Connect your wallet. Paste the OBB token address into Raydium or Jupiter, select the pair, and confirm.

4
Swap SOl for OBB

Swap your SOL for OBB on Raydium or Jupiter. Ensure you have enough SOL to cover transaction fees. Confirm the transaction to complete the swap.
